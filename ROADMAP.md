## TOP PRIORITY — Global Macro News section

Build a new section/tab called "Global" (alongside the existing Feed,
Markets, Research, Chanakya tabs). Its purpose: show WORLD news that can
move markets — the economy, geopolitics, and the stock market — not general
news. Only news that affects money belongs here.

Because Artha Terminal is India-focused, the most valuable part of this
feature is connecting global events back to Indian markets.

Build this incrementally over several days. A sensible order:

1. First, just create the new "Global" tab/section with a simple list of
   world-market news, reusing the existing news-fetching approach (public
   feeds/proxies) with global/economic sources (e.g. world business, oil,
   central banks, geopolitics). Keep it small and make sure it builds.

2. Then add "impact tags" to each news item showing what it affects:
   Equities, Oil/Energy, Currency/Rupee, Interest Rates, Geopolitics.

3. Then add a short "India impact" note on each item — one line on how the
   global event could affect Indian markets (e.g. rupee, FII flows, Nifty).

4. Then add a severity indicator (High / Medium / Low market impact),
   color-coded, so big events stand out.

Take it one step at a time — one small, working change per day.

## Things to avoid
- Do not fabricate or mock market data or news — all figures and headlines
  must come from real fetched sources. If a source can't be reached, show an
  honest "unavailable" state, never invented data.
- Keep each day's change small enough to review and build in one run.
- Do not break the existing India-focused sections; the Global section is an
  addition, not a replacement.
